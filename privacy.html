Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Dap ("we", "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use the Dap mobile application.

Information We Collect

Account Information: Username, email, and profile details you choose to provide.

Photo Check-ins: Photos you upload for daily habit check-ins. These are only visible to you and the opponent in your duel.

Device Data: Basic technical information like device type, OS version, and app performance metrics.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

• Enable habit duels and show daily check-ins

• Deliver notifications and reminders

• Improve performance, stability, and user experience

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your data. Your check-in photos are only shared with the opponent in your duel. We may share minimal data with service providers that help us deliver the app's functionality.

Data Storage & Security

We use secure, industry-standard services to store your information and protect it from unauthorized access.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.
