Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2, 2025

dap ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal data such as names, emails, or location. The app may store minimal local data required for core functionality, such as your streak, progress, or in-app preferences.

How We Use Information

Any locally stored data is used only to support app features such as tracking your streak, customizing your experience, or improving performance.

Third-Party Services

The app may use privacy-respecting analytics or crash-reporting tools. These services do not collect personal data and are used only to ensure app stability.

Data Security

We implement security measures designed to protect your information. Because most data remains on your device, you maintain full control over it.

Your Rights

You may delete the app at any time to remove all stored data. We do not maintain external accounts or cloud data tied to your identity.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in the app. Updates will always be posted here with the latest revision date.

Contact

If you have any questions, you can reach us at: support@dapapp.com
